About Greatex Febric

A Legacy of Excellence

Established in 1956, Ram Swaroop Rattan Lal (RSRL), the parent company of Greatex Synthetics Pvt. Ltd., has been a pioneer in the wholesale business of knitted and woven fabrics. Expanding into manufacturing in 1989, Greatex became a leader in customized textile solutions with advanced machinery and innovation-driven processes.

New-Age Leadership

In 2003, Mr. Ashutosh Aggrawal, a graduate of Hansraj College, joined the company. Coming from a solid commerce foundation, he brought with him a visionary approach and a tireless work ethic. His significant part in steering Greatex towards victory cannot be exaggerated. In 2016, Mr. Pranav Aggarwal, equipped with a BBA from IILM, joined the positions, including his possess brand of mastery to the company.

Uncompromising Quality & Industry Trust

Greatex is a standout manufacturer in textile industry. It has earned recognition as a Unique Gear Producer (OEM) within the article of clothing sector and is glad to be among the best manufacturers within the industry. The company has also extended its reach past residential borders, taking part in national and worldwide events and shows, setting the arrangement for worldwide growth.

Unparalleled Customization

Greatex stands out in the industry with its ability to deliver finely customized fabrics tailored to the unique needs of each client. Key customization features include designs, width, content, finishes, and strength levels. Additionally, Greatex has been a trusted manufacturer of certified recycled fabrics for years, reinforcing its commitment to sustainability and innovation.

Leading Textile Manufacturing with Global Reach

Greatex is a distinguished name in the textile industry, recognized as a Unique Gear Producer (OEM) in the apparel sector. The company has expanded beyond domestic borders, actively participating in national and international events, paving the way for global growth.

A People-Centric Approach

Greatex values its employees as its greatest asset, fostering a close-knit, family-like work environment. Many team members have been with the company for generations. Supported by a dedicated technical team, strong marketing department, and significant investment in R&D, Greatex is well-positioned to achieve its ambitious goals.
