About Greatex Febric

With more than half a century of experience and expertise in the textile and fabric manufacturing, design business we have managed to bring lot of innovative changes to the industry. Our only motto is to produce finest material in affordable rate to our customers. Our innovative mindset is the main reason why we have managed to keep up with time and did not fade away like many of our competitors.

1

In these days of heightening competition, India is seeing revolutionary changes in terms of textile industry to keep up with others in the industry. This shift in technology and methodology is going to make huge changes in the textile industry and we are proud to jump into the bandwagon and to stand still as one of the best.

2

Our workforce for us is of prime importance. We have managed to raise innovative minds as our employees and it can be evidently seen in all aspects of our products. Our HR team is comprised of experienced minds who can spot talents and as a result our manpower has become the most valuable asset of the company.

3

The technology we use in our factory is an ample example of how we roll. You can see most modern hi-tech equipment that can carry out humongous production requirements with ease and that too in short span of time. All the technology that we have deployed in the factory is also controlled by a central system to ensure that everything is aligned in place and maximum yield with no compromise in quality has been made in production.

4

Our clientele! We respect our clients a lot. We understand their requirements deep and we have always managed to safeguard their interest when it comes to production. We believe that not only corporate business but also SMEs should thrive and as a result we have tailored packages for all sorts of customers. Since we offer quality products at better rate our clients have supported us more than enough to be at the position that we are now..

5

We look forward to working with such people and we promise that there won;t be any lapse from us when it comes to quality and aesthetics of products we deliver.
